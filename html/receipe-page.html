Creamy Tomato Pasta

This is a quick and easy receipe for the yummy creamy tomato pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the heavy cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce is heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.
