MY TOP FIVE FAVORITE SHOWS

This is a list of my top 5 favorite shows

1. The Blacklist

A thrilling show about a criminal mastermind who mysteriously surrenders to the FBI.

2. The Originals

A thrilling show about the first family of vampires in New Orleans.

3. Bones

A thrilling show about a forensic anthropologist who helps solve crimes.

4. Sleepy Hollow

A thrilling show about a modern-day Ichabod Crane who is resurrected and teams up with a police lieutenant to combat supernatural forces.

5. Queen Charlotte: A Bridgerton Story

A prequel to the Bridgerton series, focusing on the early life of Queen Charlotte.
